Hephaestus is the god of artisens and craftsman. with this printer I want to be able to create cool things just like a artisen or craftsman would.

## Goals and Requirments
- standard 250mm x 250mm
- CoreXY
- Enclosed
- at least 150mm/s
- Klipper
- Under $250
- Open-Source
- Using Pico
